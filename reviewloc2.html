The Warung Cafe, Georgetown

April 28, 2024

An epic battle between hunger and taste buds. This warung serves up a feast of flavors that’ll have you cheering for every bite. Plus, the vibe? Totally chill, no actual battles here, just delicious food.

A fun spot for hearty local food with a side of charm. It's got a great vibe, but might need just a bit more variety to elevate it.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Biru Biru On The Island, Tanjung Bungah

May 26, 2024

Blue skies, blue sea, and a whole lot of blue on your plate! A cafe that feels as breezy as the ocean view, with food that’s fresh and vibrant, just like its coastal backdrop. Perfect place for a peaceful meal with a view.

Rating: ★★★★★

Rumah Kacha, Georgetown

June 28, 2024

Where homey vibes meet mouthwatering local food. From the warm, inviting ambiance to the tasty bites, this cafe feels like visiting your best friend's house, if your best friend was an excellent chef.

Feels like home, with food that makes you smile. Could do with a little more creative twist to go all the way to 5 stars.

Rating: ★★★★☆
